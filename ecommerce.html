Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build an e-commerce store?

Typical timeframes for e-commerce projects:

  • Starter Store: 2-3 weeks
  • Growth Store: 4-6 weeks
  • Enterprise Store: 8-12 weeks

The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your store, the number of products, custom functionality requirements, and content readiness.

Do I own my Shopify store?

Yes, you own your store and all its content. Shopify operates on a subscription model where you pay a monthly fee to use their platform, but the store itself, including all products, content, and customer data, belongs to you.

Any custom code or themes I develop for your store will also be your property, and you'll have full access to all files and assets created during the project.

What ongoing costs should I expect with a Shopify store?

Main ongoing costs for a Shopify store include:

  • Shopify subscription: £19 - £259 per month depending on the plan
  • Transaction fees: 2% - 0.5% per transaction if not using Shopify Payments
  • Apps: £0 - £100+ per month depending on which apps you choose
  • Domain name: Around £10 - £20 per year

I'll help you choose the most cost-effective options for your specific business needs.

Can I update my store myself after it's built?

Absolutely! One of Shopify's biggest advantages is its user-friendly admin interface. After your store is built, you'll be able to:

  • Add, edit, or remove products
  • Update content and images
  • Process orders and manage inventory
  • Create discount codes and sales
  • View analytics and reports

I also provide a comprehensive training session to ensure you're comfortable managing your store, plus documentation for future reference.
