Solicitor Website FAQ

Everything you need to know about professional legal website design and client portal systems

Do you understand legal compliance and GDPR requirements?

Absolutely. We build all legal websites with full GDPR compliance, including proper privacy policies, cookie management, data encryption, and secure client portal systems that meet Law Society guidelines for client confidentiality.

Can you integrate with our existing case management system?

Yes, we integrate with most major legal case management systems including Clio, PracticeEvolve, and LexisNexis. This ensures seamless client data flow, automated case updates, and streamlined workflow management.

How secure are the client portals for confidential documents?

Our client portals use bank-level encryption, two-factor authentication, and secure document storage. All systems are ISO 27001 compliant and regularly audited to ensure client confidentiality meets legal profession standards.

Will the website help us rank higher in local searches?

Yes, we implement comprehensive local SEO strategies including Google My Business optimization, local citations, practice area landing pages, and location-specific content to help you appear when people search for legal services in your area.

Can clients book consultations directly through the website?

Absolutely. We implement online booking systems that sync with your calendar, send automated confirmations and reminders, handle consultation fees, and can include intake forms to streamline the consultation process.

How do you showcase our legal expertise and credentials?

We create dedicated sections for your qualifications, bar admissions, professional memberships, awards, published articles, and case studies. We also implement schema markup to help search engines understand your expertise and credentials.
