At FutureLab ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide when filling out forms or contacting us.
  • Business Information: Company name, job title, and business contact information.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, products, and services.

How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services

  • To send you administrative information and updates

  • To send you marketing and promotional communications (with option to opt out)
  • To protect our website, services, and users from unauthorized activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are small files placed on your device that allow us to enhance and personalize your experience. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
