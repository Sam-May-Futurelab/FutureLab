Restaurant Website FAQ

Everything you need to know about restaurant web design and online ordering systems

Can you integrate with our existing POS system?

Yes, we integrate with most major POS systems including Square, Clover, Toast, and Lightspeed. This ensures automatic menu synchronization, real-time inventory updates, and seamless order processing without manual intervention.

How quickly can we update menu prices and items?

Menu updates are instant through your easy-to-use admin panel. Add new dishes, update prices, mark items as sold out, or add daily specials in seconds. No technical knowledge required - just log in and edit like a document.

Do you provide professional food photography services?

We partner with professional food photographers across the UK and can arrange shoots at competitive rates. Alternatively, we provide detailed guidance on capturing appetizing photos with smartphones, including lighting tips and styling advice.

Will the website work with delivery platforms like UberEats?

Absolutely. We integrate order buttons for UberEats, Deliveroo, JustEat, and other major platforms. We can also set up your own direct delivery system to reduce commission fees and build customer relationships.

How does the online reservation system work?

Customers can book tables 24/7 through your website with real-time availability checking. The system sends automatic confirmations, reminders, and can handle special requests. You control availability, party sizes, and booking restrictions through an intuitive dashboard.

Can we handle allergen information and dietary requirements?

Yes, our menu systems include comprehensive allergen marking with clear icons and detailed ingredient lists. Customers can filter by dietary requirements (vegan, gluten-free, etc.) and special dietary needs are captured during ordering and reservations.
