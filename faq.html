Project Timeline & Process

Timeline varies by project complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-3 weeks, while e-commerce sites or complex web applications may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Technical & Business Questions

Do I even need a website if I'm a small business?

Yes, even small businesses benefit greatly from having a professional website. It serves as your 24/7 digital storefront that:

  • Builds credibility with potential customers
  • Makes your business discoverable in local searches
  • Provides essential information (hours, location, services)
  • Showcases testimonials and builds trust
  • Can generate leads even outside business hours

We can create appropriately scaled solutions for businesses of all sizes.

How fast should a website load?

Ideally, websites should load in under 3 seconds. Users typically abandon sites that take longer than that to load. All our sites are built with performance optimization in mind, including compressed images, efficient code, and modern best practices to ensure fast loading times across all devices and connection speeds.

What's the difference between an eCommerce website and a regular website?

An eCommerce website includes functionality to sell products or services online, with features like:

  • Product catalogs and inventory management
  • Shopping cart and secure checkout process
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Customer account management
  • Order processing and shipping integration

Regular websites primarily provide information but don't typically include direct selling capabilities. eCommerce sites are generally more complex to build and maintain but offer the benefit of direct online sales.

My website is very old. Can you modernize it?

Definitely! Modernizing outdated websites is one of our specialties. We can transform your old site into a contemporary, mobile-friendly, and secure platform while maintaining your brand identity. This usually includes updating visual design, improving site speed, enhancing security, and ensuring mobile compatibility.

Can you redesign my existing website or app?

Yes! We specialize in redesigns and can modernize your existing site while preserving its content and improving functionality. We'll start with an audit of your current site to identify what's working, what needs improvement, and opportunities to enhance user experience.
