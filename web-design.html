Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Typical timeframes for our web design projects are:

  • Basic websites: 1-2 weeks
  • Business websites: 2-3 weeks
  • E-commerce sites: 3-5 weeks

The exact timeline depends on project complexity, content readiness, and feedback cycles. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with responsive design principles, ensuring they function and display beautifully on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile devices, we prioritize mobile optimization in everything we build.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Yes! We build most of our websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update text, images, add new pages, blog posts, and more without any technical knowledge.

We also provide training and documentation to ensure you're comfortable managing your site after launch.

Do you provide website hosting?

While we don't provide hosting services directly, we help you select the best hosting provider based on your specific needs and budget. We'll handle all the technical setup to ensure your site is properly configured, secure, and optimized for performance.

We work with trusted providers such as Cloudflare, Vercel, AWS, and traditional hosting companies depending on your project requirements.

Do your websites include SEO?

Yes, all our websites are built with technical SEO best practices including:

  • Optimized site structure and navigation
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Fast loading speeds
  • Schema markup when appropriate
  • Clean, semantic HTML
  • Optimized metadata fields
  • Image alt text capabilities
  • XML sitemap generation

We also offer more comprehensive SEO services including keyword research, content optimization, and ongoing SEO strategies as additional services.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

We offer 30 days of free support for minor adjustments after launch. For ongoing needs, we provide maintenance packages or can be available for hourly consulting and updates.

Many clients handle routine content updates themselves using the CMS, while reaching out to us for more complex design or functionality changes.
