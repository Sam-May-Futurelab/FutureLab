Frequently Asked Questions - London Web Design

How much does a website cost in London?

Website costs in London vary based on complexity and requirements. Basic websites start from £999, while e-commerce sites begin at £1,500. Custom web applications can range from £2,500-£10,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs. Unlike many London agencies that charge £3,000-£15,000, we offer competitive pricing without compromising quality.

How long does it take to build a website in London?

Most London business websites are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple brochure sites take 1-2 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms need 4-6 weeks. Timeline depends on content readiness, feedback speed, and project scope. We work efficiently to meet London's fast-paced business environment while ensuring quality isn't compromised.

Do you work with businesses outside Central London?

Yes! We serve all London boroughs including Greater London areas like Croydon, Bromley, Barnet, and Enfield. Whether you're in the City, Canary Wharf, Camden, Greenwich, or outer London, we provide the same high-quality service. Most work is done remotely with occasional face-to-face meetings in Central London when needed.

Will my website work well on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All our websites use mobile-first design, ensuring perfect functionality on smartphones and tablets. This is crucial in London where over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. We test across all major devices and browsers, and your site will load quickly even on London's busy mobile networks.

Can you help my London business rank higher on Google?

Yes! Every website includes basic SEO optimization for London searches. This includes local business schema, Google My Business optimization, location-based keywords, and technical SEO. For businesses targeting specific London areas (like "plumber in Hammersmith"), we optimize for local search terms to help you appear when customers search for services in your area.

What happens after my website launches?

You get 30 days of free support for any issues or minor changes. After that, we offer ongoing maintenance packages starting from £99/month including security updates, backups, performance monitoring, and technical support. Many London businesses choose our support packages to ensure their websites stay secure and up-to-date.

Do you provide hosting for London businesses?

Yes! We offer reliable hosting specifically optimized for UK businesses. Our hosting includes SSL certificates, daily backups, UK-based servers for fast loading in London, and 99.9% uptime guarantee. Hosting starts from £20/month and includes technical support, perfect for London businesses who want hassle-free website management.
